Issues







PRIORITY ISSUES

There are many issues facing our community, but as your representative, I have identified several key priorities that I believe require immediate attention






01

Ayushman Bharat:

Launched in 2018, Ayushman Bharat is a national health protection scheme that aims to provide health coverage to economically vulnerable families. It includes two components: the creation of Health and Wellness Centers to deliver comprehensive primary healthcare services, and the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), which provides health insurance coverage for secondary and tertiary care.



02

National Health Mission (NHM):

The NHM, launched in 2013, aims to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to rural populations. It focuses on strengthening health infrastructure, promoting preventive healthcare, and improving maternal and child health services.



03

COVID-19 Response:

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the government launched several initiatives to enhance healthcare infrastructure and services. This included the creation of COVID-19 dedicated hospitals, increased testing capacity, and the distribution of vaccines.



04

Digital Health Mission:

The government announced the National Digital Health Mission in 2020 to create a digital health ecosystem. It aims to provide a unique health ID for every Indian citizen, facilitate access to health records, and enable telemedicine services.



05

Jan Aushadhi Scheme:

This scheme aims to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices. It includes the opening of Jan Aushadhi Kendras (JAKs) where generic medicines are sold. These initiatives underline Modi's commitment to improving access to healthcare in India, particularly for marginalized and underserved communities.
